A unique feature

Every dream becomes
a short film.

After each recording, the app automatically generates a unique AI video of the dream using Runway Gen-4 Turbo.

Step 1
Claude writes the scene
A cinematic 50-word visual prompt is generated from the dream transcript. If the user has a profile photo, their face description is woven in.
Step 2
Runway renders the video
The prompt goes to Runway Gen-4 Turbo. The backend polls until ready, then downloads and stores it privately in S3.
Step 3
Moderation handled gracefully
If Runway rejects the prompt, Claude automatically rewrites it and retries. If still blocked, a quiet message appears instead of an error.

From research to code

Every threshold comes from a published paper.

The scoring logic in the app has inline citations for each decision.

VOICE
f0_std < 20 Hz scores 0.30 for Parkinson's
Monopitch is the single most specific prodromal marker, detectable 12 months before motor onset in a 7-center study. Rusz et al., Annals of Neurology, 2021
VOICE
speech_rate < 2.5 syl/sec + pause > 800 ms for Alzheimer's
Speech rate and pause duration are the strongest acoustic Alzheimer's markers per a 2024 meta-analysis of 22 studies. Saeedi et al., Journal of Prevention of Alzheimer's Disease, 2024
VOICE
energy_std < 5 dB is the primary depression discriminator
Flat loudness variation outperforms pitch in distinguishing depression. AUC 0.93, on par with clinical questionnaires. Menne et al., BMC Psychiatry, 2024
DREAM
Separate word lists for human attack and animal attack
These two attack types are the most diagnostically specific RBD dream markers. Both present together doubles the signal weight. Fasiello et al., Journal of Clinical Medicine, 2022
DREAM
Causal connective density and type-token ratio for FTD
Three linguistic features from a remote app distinguished frontotemporal dementia with 84.8% accuracy, no clinic visit required. Vonk et al., Alzheimer's and Dementia, 2025

Built with

A production-grade application on real infrastructure.

Frontend
Next.js 14 on Vercel
React, TypeScript, Tailwind. Deployed globally on Vercel. Charts in Recharts. Figtree and Noto Sans fonts.
Backend
FastAPI on AWS ECS
Async Python on Fargate. Celery and Redis for background processing. Dockerized, deployed via CodeBuild.
Database
PostgreSQL + TimescaleDB
Time-series extension for biomarker tables. Partitioned by time for fast 90 and 180-day trend queries.
Auth and Privacy
AWS Cognito
Email verification with 6-digit codes. Plain emails never stored, only SHA-256 hashes. JWT refresh tokens.
AI Stack
Claude + Whisper + Runway
Claude for dream analysis and video prompts. OpenAI Whisper for transcription. Runway Gen-4 for video generation.
Analysis
librosa + spaCy + Mann-Kendall
89 acoustic features from raw audio. spaCy for linguistic analysis. Mann-Kendall for statistically significant trend detection.
