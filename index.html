The Problem

Brain diseases are invisible
until it is too late.

What recent research shows
Monopitch is the single most specific prodromal Parkinson's voice marker, detectable 12 months before motor symptom onset. Rusz et al., Annals of Neurology, 2021
Voice-based ML biomarkers achieved AUC 0.80 for early Alzheimer's detection, outperforming traditional naming tests. Hajjar et al., Alzheimer's and Dementia: DADM, 2023
An AI voice model distinguishes depression from healthy controls with AUC 0.93, at parity with standard clinical questionnaires. Menne et al., BMC Psychiatry, 2024
Smartphone speech recordings identified Parkinson's conversion in REM sleep disorder patients with AUC 0.85. Illner et al., Movement Disorders, 2024
The gap today
55 million people worldwide have dementia. Most are diagnosed only after significant, irreversible damage has occurred.
Diagnosis requires PET scans, spinal taps, or neuropsychological tests. Most people are never screened at all.
Three linguistic features from remote app recordings can distinguish frontotemporal dementia with 84.8% accuracy and no clinic visit. Vonk et al., Alzheimer's and Dementia, 2025
If caught early, interventions that slow progression become far more effective. The detection window exists. We are simply not using it.
Research in Practice

Every threshold in the code
comes directly from a paper.

VOICE
Parkinson's: f0_std < 20 Hz triggers a 0.30 risk signal
Monopitch (reduced pitch variation) is the single most specific prodromal Parkinson's marker, detectable 12 months before motor onset. The 20 Hz cutoff is taken directly from the 7-center study. Rusz et al., Annals of Neurology, 2021
VOICE
Alzheimer's: speech_rate < 2.5 syl/sec + pause > 800 ms
Speech rate and articulation rate are the strongest acoustic Alzheimer's markers per a 2024 meta-analysis. Pause duration above 800 ms reflects the word-finding delays identified in Hajjar et al. Saeedi et al., J Prevention of Alzheimer's Disease, 2024 · Hajjar et al., Alzheimer's and Dementia: DADM, 2023
VOICE
Depression: energy_std < 5 dB is the primary discriminator
Flat loudness variation was identified as the single most important acoustic depression marker (AUC 0.93). The app weights this higher than pitch. Menne et al., BMC Psychiatry, 2024
DREAM
RBD: word lists for human attack and animal attack scenarios
The app has separate word lists for "chased, grabbed, cornered" and "wolf, bear, mauled" because Fasiello et al. identified these two attack types as the most diagnostically specific RBD dream markers. If both are present in one dream, the signal weight doubles. Fasiello et al., Journal of Clinical Medicine, 2022 · Bugalho et al., Sleep Medicine, 2024
DREAM
FTD: causal connective density and type-token ratio
Vonk et al. 2025 identified that three features from remote speech distinguish frontotemporal dementia with 84.8% accuracy. The app measures causal connective density (because, therefore, since) and lexical diversity as direct proxies. Vonk et al., Alzheimer's and Dementia, 2025
A Feature We Built

Every dream becomes
a short film.

After each recording is processed, the app automatically generates a unique AI video of the dream using Runway Gen-4 Turbo.

1
Claude writes a visual prompt from the dream transcript
Up to 50 words describing the dream scene as a cinematic video prompt. If the user has a profile photo, their face is described and added to the prompt.
2
Runway Gen-4 Turbo renders an image-to-video
The prompt is sent to Runway's API. The backend polls until the video is ready, then downloads it and stores it privately in S3.
3
Content moderation is handled automatically
If Runway rejects a prompt for content reasons, Claude rewrites it to soften the imagery and retries once. If still blocked, the app shows a gentle message instead of an error.
4
The video appears in the dream journal entry
The frontend polls for the video after processing completes. Each journal entry has its own unique dream film alongside the biomarker data.

Technical Process

What happens to your recording?

01
Upload and encryption
Audio stored in Amazon S3 with AES-256 encryption. Private by default, no public access.
02
Transcription
AWS Transcribe converts speech to text. OpenAI Whisper runs as fallback for higher accuracy.
03
Voice feature extraction
librosa analyzes raw audio across 89 dimensions: fundamental frequency, jitter, shimmer, speech rate, pause ratio, 13 MFCCs, and more.
04
Dream narrative analysis
Claude AI reads the transcript and measures coherence, emotional valence, thematic content, and lexical richness using sentence-transformers.
05
Scoring and trend detection
Scores written to a TimescaleDB time-series table. Mann-Kendall trend analysis runs over the selected window and flags significant changes.

Backend
FastAPI
Async Python on Amazon ECS Fargate. Celery and Redis handle background processing queues.
Database
PostgreSQL
TimescaleDB extension for time-series biomarker data. Private subnet, no public access.
Auth
AWS Cognito
Email verification with 6-digit codes. Plain email addresses are never stored, only SHA-256 hashes.
AI Models
Claude + Whisper
Anthropic Claude for dream interpretation. OpenAI Whisper for transcription. Runway Gen-4 for video generation.
DevOps
Docker + CodeBuild
Docker images pushed to ECR. CodeBuild pipeline deploys to ECS on each release. API keys in Secrets Manager.
