The Problem

Brain diseases are invisible
until it is too late.

What the research shows
Alzheimer's brain damage begins 15 to 20 years before any memory symptoms appear. (Bateman et al., New England Journal of Medicine, 2012)
Speech pattern changes are detectable in Parkinson's patients up to 10 years before motor symptoms. (Tsanas et al., IEEE TNSRE, 2012)
REM sleep behavior disorder predicts Parkinson's with over 90% accuracy decades in advance. (Schenck et al., Sleep, 2013)
Language decline in speech predicts Alzheimer's with 81.9% accuracy using AI models. (Fraser et al., Journal of Alzheimer's Disease, 2016)
The gap today
55 million people worldwide have dementia. Most are diagnosed only after significant damage has occurred.
Diagnosis requires PET scans, spinal taps, or neuropsychological tests. Most people never get screened at all.
If caught early, drugs that slow progression can be far more effective. The window exists. We are just not looking.
The Science Behind It

Your voice holds
measurable signals.

Voice acoustics
89 acoustic features extracted from a single recording: pitch, jitter, shimmer, speech rate, pause ratio, MFCCs, and more.
librosa (McFee et al.) + DementiaBank speech corpus
Language patterns
Reduced lexical diversity, shorter sentences, more fillers, and lower information content all precede Alzheimer's diagnosis.
Fraser et al., Journal of Alzheimer's Disease, 2016
Dream content
Narrative incoherence, emotional negativity, and self-motion themes in dreams correlate with REM sleep disorder and Parkinson's risk.
Nielsen and Stenstrom, Nature, 2005; Postuma et al., 2019
Trend detection
Mann-Kendall statistical analysis detects monotonic trends in biomarker data over 30, 90, and 180-day windows with p-value significance.
Mann (1945), Kendall (1975) nonparametric trend test

Under the Hood

What happens to your recording?

1
Upload and encryption
Audio is stored in Amazon S3 with AES-256 encryption. The file is private and never accessible publicly.
2
Transcription
AWS Transcribe converts speech to text. OpenAI Whisper runs as a fallback for higher accuracy on unclear audio.
3
Voice feature extraction (89 measurements)
librosa analyzes the raw audio: fundamental frequency, jitter, shimmer, speech rate, pause ratio, 13 MFCCs, and more.
4
Dream narrative analysis
Claude AI reads the transcript and measures coherence, emotional valence, thematic content, and lexical richness using sentence-transformers.
5
Biomarker scoring and trend detection
Scores are written to a TimescaleDB time-series table. Mann-Kendall trend analysis runs over the selected window and flags significant changes.
Built With

A production application on real infrastructure.

🌐
Frontend
Next.js 14, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS. Deployed on Vercel's global CDN. Charts built with Recharts.
⚙️
Backend API
FastAPI (async Python). Runs on Amazon ECS Fargate. Celery and Redis handle background processing.
🗄️
Database
PostgreSQL 15 with TimescaleDB extension for time-series biomarker data. RDS in a private subnet.
🔐
Auth and Privacy
AWS Cognito with email verification. Plain email addresses are never stored, only SHA-256 hashes.
🤖
AI Models
Claude (Anthropic) for dream interpretation. Whisper (OpenAI) for transcription. Runway Gen-4 for video generation.
📦
DevOps
Docker images pushed to ECR. CodeBuild pipeline deploys to ECS on every release. Secrets Manager for API keys.
