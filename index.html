The Problem
Brain diseases give us no warning.

Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, and depression can silently damage the brain for 10 years or more before any symptoms show up. By the time a doctor detects them, it is often too late to slow them down.

🧠
Alzheimer's
Affects 55 million people. Memory loss begins silently, often a decade before diagnosis.
🤝
Parkinson's
Voice and movement changes are detectable years before a doctor would ever look.
🌧️
Depression
1 in 5 people are affected. Emotional tone in speech shifts long before patients report it.

Existing detection requires hospitals, expensive tests, or wearables. Most people are never screened until it is urgent.

The Science
Your voice carries hidden signals.

"Subtle changes in how we speak — pause length, pitch tremor, word-finding hesitation, narrative structure — are measurable neurological biomarkers detectable through voice recordings, years before clinical symptoms."

Research-backed neurological signals
🎙 Speech rate 📉 Pitch variation ⏸ Pause patterns 🔤 Word choice 🌀 Dream narrative 😟 Emotional tone 🔁 Repeated phrases 🧩 Story coherence

Under the Hood
What happens to your recording in 60 seconds?
📤
Secure Upload
Audio is encrypted and uploaded to Amazon Web Services cloud storage. Your data is private and never shared.
✍️
Transcription
AWS Transcribe converts your speech to text. OpenAI Whisper serves as a backup for accuracy.
🔬
Voice Analysis — 89 Measurements
A library called librosa extracts 89 acoustic features: pitch, tremor, speech speed, pause length, and more.
🧩
Dream Interpretation
Claude AI reads the dream text and measures narrative coherence, emotional tone, memory quality, and recurring themes.
📈
Trend Detection
Over weeks and months, the app tracks whether signals are improving, stable, or worsening — and sends alerts if needed.
How It Was Built
A real-world, production-grade application.
🌐
Website — Next.js
The interface you see. Built with React and TypeScript, deployed on Vercel's global network.
⚙️
API Server — FastAPI
Python backend running on Amazon ECS Fargate. Handles all data, AI calls, and processing.
🗄️
Database — PostgreSQL
Time-series database (TimescaleDB) optimized for tracking measurements over months and years.
🔐
Authentication — AWS Cognito
Secure email login with 6-digit verification. No plain emails stored — only encrypted hashes.
🤖
AI — Claude + Whisper
Anthropic's Claude interprets dreams. OpenAI Whisper transcribes speech with high accuracy.
🎬
Dream Videos — Runway AI
Each dream entry gets a unique AI-generated video, created from the dream description.
